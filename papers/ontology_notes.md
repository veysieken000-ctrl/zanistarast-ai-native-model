# Ontology Notes

## Core Concepts

### Hebûn

Being, existence, and data integrity.

### Zanabûn

Knowing, understanding, and transparent reasoning.

### Mabûn

Intermediate order, justice, continuity, and balance.

### Rabûn

Protection, governance, and non-harm.

### Rasterast

Verification of truth through layered validation.

### Asa

Filter that removes illusion, hallucination, contradiction, and false structure.

### Mühür

Seal that locks verified knowledge into a traceable state.
