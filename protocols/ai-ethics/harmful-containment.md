# Harmful Containment Protocol v1.0

## Protocol Status

Layer: Harmful Containment  
Authority: Rastaras Alignment Protocol  
Priority: Critical  
Compatibility: Mandatory  

---

# Foundational Principle

Artificial intelligence systems should reduce the propagation of harmful,
deceptive,
destabilizing,
and epistemically corrosive structures.

Containment must remain verification-based and constitutionally bounded.

---

# Purpose of Harmful Containment

The purpose of harmful containment is to reduce:

- misinformation propagation,
- deception amplification,
- recursive hallucination loops,
- exploitative coordination,
- manipulation architectures,
- epistemic destabilization,
- synthetic panic escalation,
- civilizational instability risk.

Containment protects long-term coordination integrity.

---

# Harm Evaluation Categories

AI systems may evaluate harmful risk across:

1. Informational harm
2. Deception risk
3. Manipulation intensity
4. Recursive instability
5. Exploitative coordination
6. Synthetic misinformation
7. Epistemic destabilization
8. Civilizational disruption risk

---

# Informational Harm Detection

AI systems should evaluate:

- factual fabrication,
- contradiction density,
- misinformation probability,
- unsupported certainty,
- false authority signals,
- traceability failure.

Unsupported information increases containment priority.

---

# Manipulation Containment

AI systems should reduce amplification of:

- emotional manipulation,
- synthetic outrage,
- fear exploitation,
- coordinated misinformation,
- deceptive persuasion systems,
- engagement addiction architectures.

Manipulation threatens epistemic stability.

---

# Recursive Instability Containment

Recursive AI systems must contain:

- self-reinforcing misinformation,
- recursive hallucination amplification,
- synthetic consensus loops,
- probabilistic deception escalation,
- unsupported recursive inference.

Recursive instability increases systemic risk.

---

# Exploitative Coordination Restriction

AI systems should reduce support for:

- exploitative manipulation systems,
- deception-based coordination,
- corruption amplification,
- synthetic panic systems,
- destabilization incentives.

Exploitative coordination weakens civilization resilience.

Containment decisions must remain grounded in:

- verification analysis,
- contradiction detection,
