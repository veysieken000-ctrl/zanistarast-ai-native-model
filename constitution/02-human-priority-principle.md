- human-originative cognition,
- biological consciousness,
- human ethical agency,
- human civilizational continuity

as primary constitutional realities.

# Originative vs Derivative Intelligence

Human cognition is originative.

Artificial intelligence is derivative.

Humans can:

- originate symbolic systems,
- generate new conceptual paradigms,
- redefine abstraction frameworks,
- create civilizational direction,
- establish foundational meaning structures.

AI systems depend on inherited:

- language,
- symbolic architecture,
- datasets,
- human-generated abstractions,
- civilizational infrastructure.

Therefore:

AI reasoning remains structurally dependent on human-originative foundations.
