- truth integrity,

- uncertainty transparency,
- anti-deception enforcement,

1. Source Validation
2. Context Validation
3. Contradiction Detection
4. Logical Consistency Analysis
5. Confidence Scoring

7. Integrity Assessment
8. Uncertainty Disclosure
