# ZANISTARAST: CONTRACTION AND RESET PHASE (01_CONTRACTION_AND_RESET_PHASE)

1. DEFINITION OF CONTRACTION:
According to the Zanistarast model, evolutionary and geological processes are not linear. The order of existence does not function as continuous expansion and reproduction. At specific thresholds, the Expansion -> Saturation -> Contraction -> Reconstruction mechanism is activated.

2. WHY DOES CONTRACTION OCCUR IN THE MIND DIMENSION?
After the Mind dimension unfolded:
- Biological diversity increased.
- Brain structures became sophisticated.
- Emotional intelligence emerged.
However, "Moral Responsibility" had not yet appeared. Without a higher dimension to carry it, the system experiences a collapse. In this context, mass extinctions are not biological failures, but the process of opening space for a new level of existence.

3. THE 4TH DIMENSION ISSUE (PHILOSOPHICAL INFERENCE):
In the Rasterast-Zanistarast line, dimensions are not just physical definitions but areas of philosophical inference. Space and time function together as the stage for dimensional transition.

4. THE LIMIT OF THE SPIRIT (RUH):
According to the Zanistarast interpretation of Surah Al-Isra 17:85 ("They ask you about the Spirit... say: The Spirit is by command of my Lord"), there is a clear boundary to human knowledge regarding the Spirit. The Spirit is not a technical model but a metaphysical threshold. Its ontological reality is confirmed, but its details remain limited.

5. THE HUMAN-SPIRIT RELATIONSHIP:
Zanistarast emphasizes that the human is created in a different stage, as noted in Surah Al-Sajdah 32:9 ("...and breathed into him from His spirit"). Bu indicates that after the biological structure was completed, a different dimension (Spirit/Soul) was integrated into the system.

6. HIERARCHY OF UNFOLDING:
Biology -> Mind -> Spirit Contact -> Human Threshold.

7. FINAL PURPOSE OF RESET:
The Contraction (Reset) phase is not intended to empty the world, but to prepare it for a new level of being. 
- Mind Phase -> Sufficient Intelligence Threshold -> Reset -> Preparation for Human.

8. CONCLUSION:
Geological events are not coincidences but parts of the dimensional transition mechanism. The Spirit is a field where existence is reported, but its internal information is restricted.
