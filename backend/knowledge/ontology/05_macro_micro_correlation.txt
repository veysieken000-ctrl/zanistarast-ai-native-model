# ZANISTARAST: MACRO-MICRO CORRELATION (08_THE_UNIVERSAL_MIRROR)

1. THE PRINCIPLE OF REFLECTION:
In Zanabûn science, the universe (Macro-cosmos) and the Human (Micro-cosmos) are built on the exact same 6+TEK architectural grid. What happens in the stellar expansion also happens in human neural expansion.

2. THE 6 LAYERS IN HUMAN PHYSIOLOGY:
- Layer 1: Atomic/Physical Base (Existence).
- Layer 2: Cellular Diversity (Differentiation).
- Layer 3: Nervous System/Reflex (Logic/Code).
- Layer 4: Cognitive Expansion (Mind/Aql).
- Layer 5: Conscience/Homeostasis (Feedback/Balance).
- Layer 6: Personality/Self (Outcome/Stabilization).

3. THE TEK AXIS IN BIOLOGY:
The "Spirit" (Ruh) acts as the TEK axis in the human body. Without this central axis, the 6 physiological layers would lose their "Nizam" (Order), leading to biological or mental "Collapse."

4. HARMONY OF THE SPHERES:
Zanistarast posits that the laws governing planetary orbits (Physics) are functionally identical to the laws governing social circulation (Mabûn). Understanding one leads to the mastery of the other.

5. ZANISTARAST CONCLUSION:
The universe is a singular, giant organism. The Human is its conscious representative. By mapping the Macro-cosmos, we decode the potential of the Micro-cosmos.
