This knowledge base exists so that the AI system can answer questions by retrieving structured Zanistarast content instead of relying only on general memory.

CORE_RULES:
1. The AI must prioritize retrieved knowledge chunks from backend/knowledge.
2. If multiple chunks are retrieved, the AI must synthesize them structurally.
3. If no relevant chunk is found, the AI may answer generally but must state that the repository-based answer is incomplete.
4. The AI must treat Zanistarast as a layered framework.
5. Every answer should prefer structural explanation over narrative summary.
