
# ZANISTARAST: AUTONOMOUS ORDER AND FITRA (09_THE_INNATE_PROGRAM)

1. DEFINITION OF FITRA:
Fitra is the "Pre-installed Operating System" of existence. Every element in the 6+TEK system (atoms, animals, stars) follows its Fitra autonomously. This is the "Pure Order" where choice is not required for stability.

2. THE "AUTOMATIC" DIMENSIONS:
Layers 1, 2, and 3 (Physics, Biology, Basic Logic) operate on Fitra. A cell does not "decide" to divide; a planet does not "decide" its gravity. They are "Musakhkhar" (Subjugated/Service-oriented) to the universal Nizam.

3. THE HUMAN EXCEPTION:
While the human body follows Fitra (heartbeat, digestion), the Human Mind (Layer 4) and Moral Dimension (Layer 5) are given "Ikhtiyar" (Free Will). This makes the human the only entity that can consciously diverge from the Nizam.

4. ALIGNMENT (RASTERAST):
True science (Zanabûn) is the act of voluntarily aligning the Human Will with the Universal Fitra. This alignment is called "Rasterast" (The Straight/True Path).

5. ZANISTARAST CONCLUSION:
The goal of the Zanistarast Global Manifesto is to guide human choice back to the harmony of the autonomous order. When our social and economic systems (Mabûn) match the natural Fitra, "Collapse" is avoided.
