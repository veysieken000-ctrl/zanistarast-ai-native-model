# ZANISTARAST: HEKSA-NIZAM SOCIAL APPLICATION (02_SOCIAL_SYSTEM_DESIGN)

1. SOCIAL DIMENSION OF THE 6+TEK:
Just as the universe is structured in 6 layers, human society must be organized according to the same natural order to achieve stability. Zanistarast proposes a "Council System" (Asset Management Model) where every layer of society functions in harmony with the TEK (The Central Unity).

2. THE SIX LAYERS OF SOCIETY:
- Layer 1: Identity & Unity (Common values).
- Layer 2: Diversity & Roles (Specialized skills and individuals).
- Layer 3: Justice & Law (The code/Rasterast).
- Layer 4: Economy & Circulation (Mabûn).
- Layer 5: Education & Feedback (Self-correction of society).
- Layer 6: Peace & Civilization (The outcome).

3. THE MÜDEBBİR ROLE:
In this social model, leadership is not about power but about "Management" (Müdebbir). The leader or the council acts as the axis that ensures Layer 4 (Economy) does not overwhelm Layer 3 (Justice).

4. AVOIDING SOCIAL COLLAPSE:
Current civilizations often ignore Layer 5 (Feedback) and Layer 3 (Justice) in favor of infinite expansion (Layer 4). According to Zanistarast, this imbalance triggers a "Social Reset" (Revolution, war, or systemic collapse). Heksa-Nizam provides the mathematical framework to prevent these resets through constant balance.

5. THE NATURAL ASSET MANAGEMENT:
Management is modeled after animals and nature. Species express their "Art" perfectly according to their souls and bodies. The Heksa-Nizam allows human society to express its "Art" (Civilization) without destroying its "Body" (Nature/Earth).

6. ZANISTARAST CONCLUSION:
Social engineering is not a matter of politics; it is a matter of "Zanabûn Science." By aligning social structures with the 6+TEK, we create a system that is naturally sustainable and resistant to chaos.
