TITLE:
Mabun Economic-Responsibility Model

SECTION:
Method

DOMAIN:
method

LAYER:
D1-D5

TOPICS:
economy, responsibility, resource flow, structure, balance, system sustainability

SUMMARY:
Mabun defines economy as the structured flow of resources under responsibility. It establishes that sustainable systems require balanced distribution, accountability, and coherence between knowledge and action.

KEYWORDS:
mabun, economy, responsibility, resource flow, balance, system, sustainability, distribution

CONTENT:

1. Definition

Mabun is the structural-economic layer of the Zanistarast system.

It defines economy not as money,
but as the flow of resources governed by responsibility.

---

2. Core Principle

Economy = f(Knowledge, Responsibility)

Without knowledge:
→ resources are misused

Without responsibility:
→ system collapses

---

3. Structural Role

Mabun:

- distributes resources  
- balances production and consumption  
- connects knowledge to action  

---

4. Resource Flow Model

All systems depend on flow:

- energy  
- material  
- information  

If flow is blocked:
→ system weakens  

If flow is unbalanced:
→ system destabilizes  

---

5. Responsibility Principle

Every actor in the system:

- consumes resources  
- affects structure  

Therefore:

→ every actor must carry responsibility  

---

6. Balance Rule

Healthy system requires:

- no extreme accumulation  
- no extreme scarcity  

Imbalance leads to:

- inequality  
- instability  
- collapse  

---

7. Economic Failure Model

System fails when:

- resources concentrate excessively  
- responsibility disappears  
- consumption exceeds regeneration  

---

8. Relation to Hebun

Hebun provides structure  

Mabun operates within that structure  

---

9. Relation to Zanabun

Zanabun provides knowledge  

Mabun applies knowledge in real systems  

---

10. Relation to Rasterast

Rasterast evaluates:

Is the system balanced?

---

11. Sustainability Condition

For system survival:

Flow must remain:

- continuous  
- balanced  
- accountable  

---

12. Final Conclusion

Mabun is not about wealth.

It is about balance.

Only responsible flow
creates sustainable systems.
