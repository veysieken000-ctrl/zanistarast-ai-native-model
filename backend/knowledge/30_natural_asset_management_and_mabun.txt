# ZANISTARAST: NATURAL ASSET MANAGEMENT AND MABUN (04_ECONOMIC_CIRCULATION)

1. THE CONCEPT OF MABÛN:
In Zanistarast science, "Mabûn" represents the "Economic Circulation" within the Heksa-Nizam. It is modeled after the natural flow of energy in biological systems. Just as blood must circulate to keep the body alive, assets must circulate to keep the social body (civilization) stable.

2. MANAGEMENT VS. OWNERSHIP:
The current global crisis stems from "Absolute Ownership," which leads to stagnation and inequality. Zanistarast proposes "Natural Asset Management." 
- Principle: Humans are not the absolute owners of the Earth; they are "Müdebbir" (Managers/Governors) of the "Emanah" (Trust).
- Goal: To ensure that every unit within the 6 layers has the resources required to fulfill its function.

3. THE ANIMAL MODEL OF ART:
Animals express their existence perfectly because they follow their natural design (Fitra). A bird does not "own" the tree, but it "manages" its space to create its nest (Art). Similarly, the Heksa-Nizam Asset Management Model allows humans to produce high-level civilization and technology without "storing" resources in a way that causes systemic "Contraction."

4. THE COUNCIL SYSTEM:
The management of these assets is handled through a "Council System." This is a decentralized yet unified decision-making body that ensures:
- Layer 4 (Production) serves the whole.
- Layer 3 (Justice) prevents exploitation.
- Layer 6 (Outcome) creates a "Beautiful World" for all species.

5. PREVENTING ECONOMIC RESET:
When assets stop circulating and accumulate at a single point, the system loses its "6+TEK" balance. This triggers an economic "Collapse/Reset." Mabûn prevents this by ensuring continuous flow based on the "Need and Function" principle.

6. ZANISTARAST CONCLUSION:
Economy is not about money; it is about the "Nizam" (Order) of existence. By adopting the Natural Asset Management model, we move from a "Chaos-based" economy to a "Zanabûn-based" scientific civilization.
