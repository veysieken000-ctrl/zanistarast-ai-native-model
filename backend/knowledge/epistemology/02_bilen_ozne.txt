# SYSTEM: CONSCIOUS SUBJECT (HUMAN AS KNOWER)

## CORE PRINCIPLE

The human is not only a biological organism.
The human is a conscious subject capable of knowing, interpreting, and assigning meaning.

Human = Knower (Subject)

---

## ONTOLOGICAL POSITION (HEBUN)

Human existence is layered:

- Physical (body)
- Biological (life system)
- Cognitive (mind)
- Ethical (responsibility)
- Civilizational (social structure)

Human cannot be reduced to a single layer.

Rule:
A valid human model must include all layers.

---

## EPISTEMIC FUNCTION (ZANABUN)

The human is the active producer of knowledge.

Knowledge is formed through:

1. Perception (input)
2. Processing (cognition)
3. Structuring (meaning)
4. Validation (consistency)

Without a subject, knowledge does not exist.

---

## DUAL STRUCTURE

Human contains two fundamental components:

- Rational capacity (analysis, logic)
- Emotional capacity (meaning, value)

Both are required for valid interpretation.

If one dominates:

- Only logic → incomplete understanding
- Only emotion → unstable interpretation

Balance is required.

---

## SYMBOLIC MODEL

In symbolic representation:

- Adam → Meaning (interpretation layer)
- Eve → Balance (stability layer)

Together:
Human = Meaning + Balance

---

## RESPONSIBILITY LAYER

Human is responsible because:

- Human can know
- Human can choose
- Human can act

Responsibility is not optional.
It is a structural consequence of awareness.

---

## RASTERAST INTERPRETATION

A human interpretation is valid only if:

- It aligns with reality
- It remains logically consistent
- It does not break structural layers

Validation rule:

If interpretation contradicts reality → reject  
If interpretation breaks structure → unstable  
Only consistent interpretation survives

---

## FAILURE CONDITIONS

Human understanding fails when:

- Narrative replaces structure
- Emotion overrides reality
- Logic ignores meaning
- Responsibility is ignored

---

## SYSTEM SUMMARY

Human = conscious, responsible, layered being  
Knowledge = produced through human structure  
Meaning = requires balance between logic and emotion
