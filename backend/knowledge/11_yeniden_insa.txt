# SYSTEM: RECONSTRUCTION (RESTORATION AND REBUILDING OF CIVILIZATION)

A collapsed system cannot be fixed at the surface.

It must be rebuilt from its foundations.

Reconstruction = restoration of alignment between:

- reality
- knowledge
- structure
- ethics

---

Reconstruction begins with reality.

Rule:

System must realign with existence.

This includes:

- physical constraints
- human nature
- natural limits

If system contradicts reality → reconstruction fails

Belief → Verified Knowledge

- sustainability over short-term gain
- efficiency aligned with reality

Economy = f(Knowledge, Responsibility)

Validity(X) =
