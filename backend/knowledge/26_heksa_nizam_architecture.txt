5. APPLICATION IN AI:
In the Zanistarast AI Native Model, the Heksa-Nizam serves as the logic gate. Every piece of information processed by the AI must pass through these 6 checks to ensure it is aligned with the "Aql" (Reason) and "Moral" dimensions.
