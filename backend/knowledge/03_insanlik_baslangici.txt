History does not begin with events.

History = f(Conscious Subject)

- Physical → Biological → Cognitive (Human)

- Awareness
- Interpretation
- Responsibility

A new layer must not violate lower layers.

- Meaning-making

- Knowledge becomes possible

- Structured interpretation of events

Formula:
History = f(Meaning, Memory, Structure)
