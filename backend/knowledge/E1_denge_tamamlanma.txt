# SYSTEM: COMPLETION OF BALANCE (INTEGRATION OF ETHICS, LAW, AND POWER)

## CORE PRINCIPLE

A stable civilization requires balance.

Balance = integration of:

- ethics
- law
- power
- knowledge

No single component can dominate.

---

## ONTOLOGICAL POSITION (HEBUN)

Balance is a higher-order structural state.

Hierarchy:

Existence → Knowledge → Law → Institution → Ethics → Balance

Balance integrates all previous layers without contradiction.

Rule:

No layer can dominate the system.
All layers must remain consistent.

---

It becomes:

- integrated

- system-wide

Knowledge is no longer fragmented.

- stability
