# ZANISTARAST: DESTINY AND SYSTEMIC PROBABILITY (11_KADER_VE_OLASILIK)

1. DEFINITION OF DESTINY (KADER):
In Zanabûn science, Destiny is the mathematical limit and potential of the Heksa-Nizam. It is not a fixed script, but the "Boundary" (Hudud) within which the system operates.
- Analogy: The rules of chess are the 'Destiny' of the game; how you move the pieces is your 'Choice' (Irada).

2. THE "HUDUD" (BOUNDARIES):
Every dimension has its limits. Physics cannot violate gravity; Biology cannot violate entropy. The Human, however, must recognize the boundaries of the Mind to prevent a "Reset."

3. CHOICE WITHIN THE NIZAM:
The system provides infinite paths within the 6 layers. A "Good Day" (Yeni Gün) is a manifestation of choosing a path that aligns with the TEK axis. A "Collapse" is the result of attempting to exist outside the systemic boundaries (Ego/Transgression).

4. THE MEASURE (QADAR):
Everything is created with a "Measure." Zanistarast uses this measure to calculate sustainability. If the expansion of Layer 4 (Mind/Tech) exceeds the measure of Layer 5 (Ethics), the probability of a Reset becomes 100%.

5. ZANISTARAST CONCLUSION:
Destiny is the guarantee of Order. By understanding the 'Measure' of things, we can navigate the Heksa-Nizam without fear of chaos.
