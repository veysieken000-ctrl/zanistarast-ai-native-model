About Zanistarast

Zanistarast is a structured, testable framework for modeling natural, computational, and human systems through coherence, validation, and integration.

What It Is

Zanistarast is not presented as ideology, mythology, or narrative. It is proposed as a structural model designed for analysis, comparison, validation, and civilizational reasoning.

Purpose

To create a coherent framework that explains how systems emerge, organize, stabilize, and collapse across multiple domains.

Orientation

Truth-centered, structure-centered, and validation-centered. The framework prioritizes coherence over noise and evidence over ideology.

Core Principles

Truth > Power

Structure > Narrative

Evidence > Ideology

Framework Layers

Hebûn — Ontology / Being

Zanabûn — Epistemology / Knowledge

Mabûn — Structure / Economy / Responsibility

Rasterast — Method / Correct Interpretation

Zanistarast — Integrative System Layer

Scientific Position

The framework is presented as structured, testable, non-ideological, machine-readable, and cross-domain. Its goal is not rhetorical persuasion, but structural clarity and falsifiable coherence.

What This Site Provides

This site serves as the public access point for the Zanistarast system: overview, paper, graph, interactive AI interface, manifesto, and repositories.
