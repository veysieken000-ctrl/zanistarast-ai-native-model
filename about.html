About Zanistarast

Zanistarast Scientific Synthesis is presented as a natural science framework for ontology, epistemology, validation, structural order, and civilizational continuity.

What It Is

Zanistarast is not presented here as ideology, myth, rhetoric, or identity ornament. It is presented as a structured scientific model for examining how reality, knowledge, validation, and civilizational systems must align if coherent order is to exist.

Natural Science Position

Zanistarast defines itself as a natural science framework in the extended sense: it begins from reality, structure, causality, and validation rather than from human preference. It asks what kind of order must emerge when being, knowledge, and structural coherence are correctly understood.

Core Principle

Science does not change according to the human. The human must learn to align with science. For this reason, Zanistarast rejects the idea that truth should be revised to fit desire, ideology, or convenience. Instead, human systems, institutions, and forms of life must be reorganized in accordance with reality.

Truth

Truth is the governing constraint. A system that breaks from truth loses legitimacy, direction, and continuity.

Structure

Structure is the condition of endurance. Without structure, knowledge fragments and civilization decays into noise.

Validation

Validation protects the system from illusion, distortion, and narrative corruption. A claim without validation has no stable place in the framework.

Continuity

Continuity emerges only when ontology, knowledge, method, and responsibility remain coherent across time.

Framework Layers

Hebûn — the ontological layer of being.

Zanabûn — the epistemological layer of knowledge.

Mabûn — the structural layer of responsibility, circulation, and order.

Rasterast — the method layer of correct reading and validation.

Zanistarast — the synthesis layer integrating the whole system.

6+TEK / 6+Ehad Principle

Within this framework, 6+TEK / 6+Ehad is not treated as an arbitrary slogan. It is presented as a structural principle of order: a valid system must know its governing unity, its organizing relations, and the limits under which coherence can be sustained.

Newroza-Kawa Civilization

The Newroza-Kawa Civilization is not introduced as a decorative narrative layer placed on top of the system. It is presented as the civilizational expression of coherent order when ontology, knowledge, validation, and responsibility are correctly aligned. In this sense, it appears not as a cultural preference alone, but as a natural requirement of structural continuity.

Scientific Aim

The aim of Zanistarast is to make systems readable, testable, comparable, and structurally accountable across domains. It is meant for analysis and disciplined synthesis, not for rhetorical allegiance.
