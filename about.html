About Zanistarast

A structural framework for reality, knowledge, and civilization.

What It Is

Zanistarast is a structured, falsifiable framework that connects ontology, epistemology, coherence, validation, and civilizational integration into a single model.

What It Rejects

It is not based on ideology, personality, or narrative persuasion. It rejects disconnected abstraction, incoherent systems, and unfalsifiable claims.

What It Proposes

It proposes that durable civilization depends on alignment between reality, knowledge, structure, and method. When these layers detach, systemic collapse begins.

Main Components

Ontological ground, epistemic validation, structural economy, coherence filtering, and integrative civilizational output.

Why It Matters

This framework is designed not only to be read, but to be explored, tested, formalized, and expanded across repositories, diagrams, papers, and AI interfaces.
