Explanation
Zanistarast suggests that all systems — natural, computational, and social — follow similar layered principles:
Unity (origin)
Validation (consistency)
Circulation (balance)
Function (alignment)
Outcome (stability or collapse)
A system survives not by perfection,
but by its ability to detect, correct, and realign itself.
